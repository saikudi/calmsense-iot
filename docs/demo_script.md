# Demo Script (5 minutes max)

1. Show the hardware setup and explain each sensor.
2. Power on the TTGO and show baseline calibration (30 seconds).
3. Show ThingSpeak dashboard fields and graphs.
4. Demonstrate motion change by moving the device and show stress score rise.
5. Demonstrate touch sensor by tapping repeatedly and show touch rate increase.
6. Change lighting (cover the light sensor) and show light penalty effect.
7. Explain LED and buzzer alerts for medium and high stress states.
8. Wrap up with future improvements.
